Our Story

A space to feel
beautifully yourself.

Founded by Rebecca in 2015, Becks Hairdresser was born from a simple belief — that every visit to the salon should feel like a retreat. Set in the heart of London, our studio blends expert technique with a deeply personal touch.

We take time to listen, to understand your lifestyle and vision, and to craft results that last. Whether you're seeking a bold transformation or a subtle refresh, you're in the finest hands.

Luxury colour specialists using Aveda & Olaplex

Bespoke consultations for every appointment

Complimentary scalp & hair health analysis

Sustainable & cruelty-free products only
